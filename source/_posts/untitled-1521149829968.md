This is for mysql or MariaDB
```sql
select * from (select 'CABECERA' as cabecera
UNION
select apellido_nombre from db.persona) a
limit 10
INTO OUTFILE 'e:\persona.csv'
FIELDS TERMINATED BY ','
```
